BridgeUTK is an official chapter of BridgeUSA. We strive to create a solution-oriented, multipartisan political culture on campus. We envision a future of civilized, and constructive dialogue with a wide diversity of viewpoints. As future leaders, change starts with us. Our goal is not to force agreement or create centrists, but to encourage young minds to think about how to create a better political future.
